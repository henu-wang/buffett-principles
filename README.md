# Warren Buffett Investment Principles Explorer

An interactive single-page application exploring 20 key investment principles from Warren Buffett, the Oracle of Omaha.

**Live Site:** [https://henu-wang.github.io/buffett-principles/](https://henu-wang.github.io/buffett-principles/)

## Features

- **20 Core Principles** - Detailed explanations with real-world examples and application tips
- **Category Filtering** - Value Investing, Risk Management, Business Quality, Market Psychology, Personal Growth
- **Search** - Instantly find principles by keyword
- **Quiz Mode** - Test your knowledge with 10 questions
- **Investment Timeline** - Key decisions from 1956 to present
- **Dark Theme** - Elegant Berkshire Hathaway-inspired design with gold accents
- **Mobile Responsive** - Works beautifully on all devices

## Categories

| Category | Principles |
|----------|-----------|
| Value Investing | Circle of Competence, Intrinsic Value, Margin of Safety, Long-Term Holding |
| Risk Management | Never Lose Money, Be Fearful When Others Are Greedy, Avoid Leverage, Risk vs Uncertainty |
| Business Quality | Economic Moats, Owner-Oriented Management, Simple Businesses, Pricing Power |
| Market Psychology | Mr. Market, Avoid the Herd, Patience & Discipline, Emotional Control |
| Personal Growth | Continuous Learning, Reputation, Focus Over Diversification, Think Independently |
